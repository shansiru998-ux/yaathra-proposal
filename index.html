The Event

What is
YaaTHRA

"යාත්‍රා" YaaTHRA is the first edition of a unique live music experience dedicated to celebrating the timeless beauty of Sri Lankan cinema music. Blending nostalgia, emotion, and storytelling, the event brings to life the melodies that have shaped generations through film.

From classic hits to modern cinematic compositions, it captures the true essence and soul of Sri Lankan movie soundtracks in an immersive live setting.

Unlike conventional concerts, YaaTHRA is designed to recreate the feeling of cinema through music — where every performance unfolds like a scene, evoking memories, emotions, and stories familiar to every listener.

YaaTHRA brings Sri Lankan cinema music to life: a captivating live experience where iconic film melodies, emotion, and storytelling blend into one cinematic journey. This first edition sets the tone for a signature series of unforgettable musical moments.

The Creative Force

Produced By

Nirosha Virajini

Nirosha Virajini is a celebrated and versatile icon in Sri Lankan music, with a distinguished career spanning more than three decades as a vocalist, composer, music director, voice trainer, instructor, and reality show judge. Classically trained in Karnataka and Hindustani music, with studies at Kalakshetra, Chennai and Bhatkhande (Lucknow University), and honoured with the prestigious Visharada qualification, she brings deep artistic and academic excellence to her work.

Renowned for her multilingual artistry, she has performed in Sinhala, Tamil, Hindi, Malayalam, Telugu, English, Dhivehi, as well as Japanese and Chinese for international cultural events.

Beyond performance, she has made a lasting impact through Beat of the Heart Academy, mentoring aspiring artists. Recognised with honours including Presidential, Sarasavi, Sumathi, Derana, Raigam, and SLIM Nielsen awards, Nirosha Virajini stands as a respected cultural icon whose legacy blends classical excellence, creative innovation, and dedication to nurturing future talent.

Janaki Wijerathne

Janaki Wijerathne is a Sri Lankan actress and television presenter known for her versatile work across performance, production, and media leadership. She has international experience, including consulting for a Japanese company, and has produced many tele dramas since beginning her production journey. She strengthened her presence in major live entertainment through her production of Shreya Ghosal's live concert in Sri Lanka.

In addition to her creative contributions, Janaki has held influential roles in media operations. She started her career in the UK as Head of the Sinhala channel at Vectone TV and later worked with Lyca Mobile and related productions, including a consulting role for Lyca Group in Sri Lanka. She currently serves in senior leadership positions as Vice President of both Lyca Productions and Savoy Films and Theatre.

Janaki holds an MBA (UK) and a BA (Hons) in Travel & Tourism (UK), combining her academic background with extensive experience in the entertainment and media industry.
